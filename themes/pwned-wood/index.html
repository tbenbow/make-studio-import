The Pivot

Look, we saw the writing on the wall. Or more accurately, we saw AI writing the code on the wall. Every developer's worst nightmare turned into our best career decision.

We traded git commits for concrete footings. Swapped our standing desks for sawhorses. Replaced code reviews with building inspections.

Turns out, 15 years of obsessing over architecture, structural integrity, and clean execution translates pretty well when the architecture is actual architecture.

The bugs are bigger now (literal beetles), but at least they don't ship to production at 3AM.
