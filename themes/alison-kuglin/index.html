The Material Story

From Reclaimed
to Remarkable

In a world of mass-produced jewelry, Allison transforms what others discard into something extraordinary. Recycled metals from her father's job site. Thrifted treasures given new purpose. Wire shaped by hand into wearable art.

Every material has a past — and in Allison's hands, a luminous future. Stones are sourced from a team of sisters in the mountains, and metals come from trusted US companies committed to sustainable practices.

Recycled Metals

Mountain Stones

Custom Pieces

Made for You,
Shaped by Story

Beyond our seasonal and signature collections, Allison accepts a limited number of custom commissions each month. Whether it's a reimagined heirloom or something entirely new, every custom piece begins with conversation.

  • Choose your metals — recycled silver, copper, or bronze
  • Select stones from our ethically sourced collection
  • Rings, earrings, necklaces, or something entirely your own
  • Typical turnaround of 4–6 weeks
01

Share Your Vision

Tell Allison what you're dreaming of — a sketch, a feeling, a memory to carry with you.

02

Design Together

Allison sketches options and selects materials. You'll approve the direction before any metal is shaped.

03

Handcraft & Ship

Your piece is made entirely by hand in the Hackensack workshop, then carefully packaged and sent to you.
